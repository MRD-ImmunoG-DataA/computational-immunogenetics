## Research Focus

This repository focuses on computational and data-driven approaches to understanding the immune system, particularly the genetic, structural, and functional properties of immune receptors.

Major research directions include:

- Computational immunology
- Immunogenetics
- Structural immunology
- T-cell receptor (TCR) analysis
- B-cell receptor (BCR) analysis
- Immune receptor repertoire analysis
- Cancer immunology
- Autoimmune diseases
- Neuroimmunology
- Evolutionary immunology
- Systems immunology
- Molecular interaction analysis
- Immunoinformatics

The long-term objective is to investigate how immune receptor diversity, structural geometry, and genetic variation influence immune recognition and immune-related diseases.
